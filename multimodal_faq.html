Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use MAD database free of charge?
A: MAD database is free of charge for research purposes only. For commercial applications, please contact us.

Q: Can I modify and redistribute MAD database?
A: Unfortunately, NO. Please send us your suggestions and we will try to add them in future releases. If it is a major change, please contact us, we are always eager to explore collaborations (e.g., writing a joint proposal).
